== Biography ==

Clara was born in 1901. She was the daughter of [[Bayley-335|Isaiah Bayley]] and [[Fawdry-10|Clara Fawdry]].  She passed away in 1981.<ref>A source for this information is needed.</ref>



Clara was born on 23 March 1901 in Llanerch, Delaware County, Pennsylvania, USA.

Clara departed Liverpool, England and arrived in New York, New York on 18 March 1910.<ref>'''Arrival''': New York, Passenger Lists, 1820-1957, Year: 1910.</ref>

In the 1910 census Clara was living in Haddon, Camden, New Jersey, USA. Marital Status: Single. Relation to Head of House: Daughter.<ref>'''1910 Census''': 1910 United States Federal Census,<br/>
Year: 1910; Census Place: Haddon, Camden, New Jersey; Roll: T624_874; Page: 8B; Enumeration District: 0102; FHL microfilm: 1374887.</ref>

In the 1911 census Clara was living in Hendon, Middlesex, England. Relation to Head of House: Daughter.<ref>'''1911 Census''': [https://www.familysearch.org/search/record/results?q.givenName=Clara&q.surname=Bayley&q.birthLikeDate.from=1900&q.birthLikeDate.to=1902&q.birthLikePlace=Llanerch,%20Delaware%20County,%20Pennsylvania,%20USA.%20&q.residenceDate.from=1911&q.residenceDate.to=1911&q.residencePlace=Hendon,%20Middlesex,%20England.%20&f.collectionId=1921547 1911 Census Returns of England and Wales], original data from [https://discovery.nationalarchives.gov.uk/results/r?id=C13339&_q=RG14+7125 The National Archives], reference: Class: RG14; Piece: 7125; Schedule Number: 10.</ref>

Clara departed Southampton, Hampshire, England on 9 October 1926. SS LANCASTRIA.

Clara arrived in New York, USA on 18 October 1926. SS LANCASTRIA. Hotel Brighton Atlantic City New Jersey.

Clara departed New York, New York, United States and arrived in Southampton, England on 17 May 1927.<ref>'''Arrival''': UK, Incoming Passenger Lists, 1878-1960,<br/>
Class: BT26; Piece: 855; Item: 1.</ref>

Clara married in September 1927 in Woolwich, Kent, England.<ref>'''Marriage''': England & Wales, Marriage Index, 1916-2005.</ref>
