Henry Berris Odgers was born on 16 March 1896 in Nelson, Lancashire, England, United Kingdom.
Henry married [[Avis-319|Emily Avis]] (1894 - 1965) in 30 September 1916 in Burnley, Lancashire.
Their son was [[Odgers-510|Henry Malcolm Odgers]] (1917 - 2009).
In 1939, Henry was living in Lancashire, England. .
 
Henry died on ~1 December 1968 in Newhaven, Sussex, England aged 72.
